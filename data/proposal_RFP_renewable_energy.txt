PROPOSAL – RFP 24-067
Submitted by: GreenVision Technologies

Subject: Implementation of Predictive Maintenance AI System for Solar Farms

Summary:
This proposal details the design and deployment of a machine learning-based platform capable of monitoring and predicting equipment failures across distributed solar installations.

Objectives:
- Integrate IoT sensors for real-time data collection.
- Use deep learning models for fault detection.
- Provide an operator dashboard with alerts and analytics.

Timeline: 6 months from project kickoff.
Budget Estimate: €245,000.
Contact: Clara Gómez – Project Lead.
