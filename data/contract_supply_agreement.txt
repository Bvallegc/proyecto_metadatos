CONTRACT AGREEMENT

This Service Supply Agreement (“Agreement”) is entered into on February 12, 2024, between TechNova Consulting (“Provider”) and Global Infrastructure Ltd. (“Client”).

1. Scope of Work
   The Provider shall deliver technical advisory services related to AI-based document automation tools for internal process optimization.

2. Deliverables
   Weekly progress reports, one final implementation report, and staff training.

3. Duration
   From February 12, 2024 to July 12, 2024, unless extended by mutual agreement.

4. Confidentiality
   Both parties agree not to disclose confidential information obtained during the execution of this agreement.

5. Signatures
   Signed by representatives of both parties on the date indicated above.
