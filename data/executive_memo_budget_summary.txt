MEMORANDUM  
To: Executive Committee  
From: Financial Strategy Division  
Date: April 10, 2024  
Subject: Q1 Budget Utilization and Forecast

Summary:
Overall expenditure for AI innovation programs reached 78% of the allocated budget by end of Q1. Primary cost drivers include cloud hosting (35%) and model fine-tuning (28%).

Recommendations:
- Reassess contract with Azure AI Studio for potential reserved-instance pricing.
- Introduce approval workflow for compute-intensive experiments.
- Prepare detailed forecast for Q2 with updated project milestones.
